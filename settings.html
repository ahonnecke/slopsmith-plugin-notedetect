Mic capture latency —

Round-trip from speaker to onset detection. Hardware-dependent (USB ADC, OS audio stack, browser MediaStream). Subtracted from highway timing labels so what you see is your real offset, not the hardware delay. Calibrate once per setup; the value persists across sessions and songs.

Four phases: two reaction-time baselines (no instrument), then visual + audio bass tests. Calibration locks after 3 high-confidence audio runs converge within ±10 ms.
